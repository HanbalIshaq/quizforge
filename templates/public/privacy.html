Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-15

1. What we collect

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and a salted bcrypt hash of your password. When you create quizzes, we store the quiz content, settings, and the responses submitted to it. When students take quizzes, we may store their name, email (if your quiz collects it), their answers, attempt timestamps, IP address (for fraud/abuse prevention), and integrity-violation events (tab switches, paste attempts, etc.) when anti-cheating is enabled.

2. Why we collect it

Solely to operate the service: authenticate accounts, render quizzes, grade responses, generate reports, and prevent abuse. We do not sell your data, ever. We do not use student responses to train AI models.

3. Where it\'s stored

Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database under our or your operator\'s control. If you use the hosted QuizForge service, data is stored with reputable cloud providers (Neon / Render). If you self-host, the data never leaves your servers.

4. Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except: (a) payment processors when you upgrade (Stripe), (b) law enforcement when legally compelled. We never sell data to advertisers.

5. AI features

When you use the optional AI quiz generator, the text you submit is sent to an LLM API provider (e.g. Anthropic) under their privacy terms, processed once, and not stored by them long-term. Self-hosters can disable this feature.

6. Deletion

You can delete any quiz or individual response from your admin dashboard. Account deletion is available on request — email us. Self-hosters control their own retention.

7. Cookies

We use a single session cookie for login state. No third-party trackers, no advertising cookies, no analytics by default.

8. Contact

For privacy questions email privacy@quizforge.example.
