About QuizForge

QuizForge is built for teachers, trainers and certification bodies who are tired of paying for three different tools to do one job. We rolled the best of Quizizz (live game-show), ClassMarker (formal exams + certificates) and Mentimeter (live polls) into a single platform — at a fraction of the price.

Our mission

Make professional online assessment affordable, beautiful and private. Your students\' answers belong on your servers, not a third-party warehouse.

Why "open-core"

We give the core code away because we believe in software you actually own. Customers pay us for the hosted version (no setup hassle, automatic updates), branding, integrations and priority support.

What makes QuizForge different

  • ✅ One tool, three jobs — exams, live polls, and surveys in a single platform.
  • ✅ Self-hostable — install it on your own server in 5 minutes (PostgreSQL + Python).
  • ✅ Modern UI — Tailwind-based design that doesn\'t look like it\'s from 2012 (looking at you, ClassMarker).
  • ✅ AI built in — paste a document, get questions. No external service needed at the student level.
  • ✅ Anti-cheating that respects students — tab-switch & copy-paste detection without invasive webcam monitoring (unless you opt in).
  • ✅ Pay only when you grow — free forever for solo use. No "trial" trap.
